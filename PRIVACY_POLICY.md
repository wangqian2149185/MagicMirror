# MagicMirror Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

MagicMirror is a guided self-reflection app. Its results are for reference only and are not medical, psychological, legal, financial, or professional advice.

## Data Stored On Device

MagicMirror stores app settings, interview progress, generated reports, and optional API configuration locally on your device. If you enter an API key, the key is stored locally on your device so the app can call the selected model provider.

## Optional Model Provider Requests

If you choose a paid or custom model provider and enter an API key, MagicMirror sends the text you provide in the interview, scoring answers, and relevant app prompts to the selected provider to generate summaries, question rephrases, calibration predictions, and reports.

If you choose Free Local mode or leave the API key blank, MagicMirror uses local fallback logic and does not require a cloud model provider.

MagicMirror does not operate the third-party model providers. Their handling of requests is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## Voice Input

Voice input is optional. When you tap the voice input button, the app requests microphone and speech-recognition permission so spoken answers can be converted into text. The resulting text may be stored locally as part of your interview answer and, if you use a cloud model provider, may be included in model requests.

## Clipboard

When you tap Copy Markdown, MagicMirror writes the generated report text to your device clipboard.

## Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

MagicMirror does not include advertising, third-party tracking, or analytics SDKs.

## Data Deletion

You can clear answers and generated reports inside the app. You can also delete the app from your device to remove locally stored app data.
